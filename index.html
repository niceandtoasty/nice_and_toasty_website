Nice and Toasty is a party game that turns your friendly banter into a competition.

Whether you spill some tea with Truth, test your knowledge with Pop Quiz, expose each other with Spotlight, or try your skills with Challenge, you're in for a toasty treat!

Want to know more?

Nice and Toasty is still in development, but we put answers to some of our most frequently asked questions below.

It's pretty easy! Your goal is to gather the most points by answering questions your group pulls from the deck. In the process, you'll probably get a lot of amusing stories and moments out of your group. Pretty sweet!

Here's a little step-by-step:

  • Pull a card from the deck. There are 4 types of cards in the deck: Truth, Pop Quiz, Spotlight, and Challenge.
  • Answer the prompt on the card. Most cards in the deck involve the entire group!
  • Assign points to players that answer correctly.

The age range is 17+, and the game needs 3 or more players! From experience though, 4 to 10 players feels like the best range.

We're still figuring out what we should include in the box to make the game play as fun as possible! However, you're definitely getting these:

  • 30 Truth cards
  • 30 Pop Quiz cards
  • 30 Spotlight cards
  • 10 Challenge cards
  • A rule card

We're aiming to finish the development of the card game by June 2024. In terms of when you can get a copy, it really boils down to how much interest Nice and Toasty gets! More interest allows us to scale our production so that we can send everyone a copy at an affordable price.

We have tentative plans to run a Kickstarter campaign for Nice and Toasty towards the end of summer in 2024. That'll be your earliest opportunity to grab a copy of the game!
